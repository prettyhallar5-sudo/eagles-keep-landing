About

Eagles Keep Counseling is a wellness practice rooted in mental health, somatic care, and culturally aware, trauma-informed healing. Our work blends licensed clinical counseling with holistic wellness practices — helping people pursue transformation without guilt, shame, or judgment.

"Sankofa" — go back and get it. We believe healing means revisiting the past for wisdom, reclaiming what serves us, and moving forward with grace. You are always welcome to begin again.

Services

  • Individual Counseling — trauma-informed, EMDR-informed talk therapy for adults and adolescents (13+).
  • Somatic & Yoga-Informed Wellness — body-based practices to support nervous system regulation and healing.
  • Corporate & Educator Wellness — consulting, workshops, and training for organizations, schools, and universities.
  • Wellness Products — merchandise and tools designed to support your everyday healing practice.

Programs for Schools & Educators

Trauma-Informed Educator Training

$300–$600 per seat · flat rate available for whole-staff bookings

A live, interactive professional development course for teachers and school staff, grounded in SAMHSA's trauma-informed framework and the National Child Traumatic Stress Network's system model for trauma-informed schools. Educators learn to recognize the signs of trauma, respond in ways that avoid re-traumatizing students, and apply classroom strategies built on safety, trust, and student voice — taught by a licensed, trauma- and EMDR-informed clinician.

Contact hours may qualify for district professional-enrichment credit depending on local policy; this course does not currently issue official PLU/CEU credit.

Educational Trauma Debrief

Custom quote, based on group size & format

Structured, psychological-first-aid-informed support for school staff following a crisis or traumatic incident in the school community. This is deliberately designed as ongoing psychoeducational staff support rather than a single mandated "debriefing" session — research shows rigid one-time debriefing models can be ineffective or even counterproductive. Sessions are scaled to your school's needs and aligned with recognized school crisis-response practice.
